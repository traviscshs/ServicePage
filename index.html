CS2

CSHS' Mentorship Project

CS² (CS-squared) is CSHS’ mentorship program, where members of CSHS are matched with other students in CS and other STEM-related subjects as mentors, serving as tutors and advisors!

CSHS Mentors are able to help in all STEM-related and Computer Science Subjects, serving as advisors and mentors to other students. If you are interested in being mentored, please complete the form below!

Please email CS2@thscshs.org if you have questions!
